About Me

Hi, and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Sara Rutherfurd, and I am a software engineer based in Irvine, CA. I graduated from California State University Fullerton in January 2023 with a BSc in Computer Science and a GPA of 3.4. Overall, my coursework at CSUF provided me with a well-rounded education in computer science, with a particular emphasis on software engineering. My journey in software engineering began with the hands-on experience I gained while working on various software projects for my university classes. Since graduating, I have been honing my skills in front-end web development, which has become my primary focus. I am currently working on various side projects to further develop my skills and pursue my interests. I believe in continuous learning and improvement, and I am always seeking new challenges to broaden my horizons. In my free time, I enjoy walking, anime, and spending time with my family, friends, and pets. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my projects and learning more about me as a software engineer.
